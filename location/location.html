How to Get Here with Easy 5 Steps:

Step 1: Travel to Camarines Norte.

Step 2: From any point in Camarines Norte, take bus/jeep/tricycle bound to Daet.

Step 3: From Daet, take tricycle to Mercedes Jeep Terminal, and take jeep to go to Mercedes Fishport.

Step 4: From Mercedes Fishport, take boat to Manguisoc.

Step 5: From Manguisoc, take tricycle to Salazar Cottages.
